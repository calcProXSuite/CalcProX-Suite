GST - Goods and Services Tax

GST is the Goods and Services Tax, an indirect tax that came into effect in India on July 1st, 2017. It replaced multiple cascading taxes imposed by the central and state governments. GST is applicable to all products and services sold in India and is divided into CGST, SGST, and IGST depending on inter- or intra-state transactions.

How can you calculate GST with this tool?

Step 1: Enter the price of the goods or services.
Step 2: Choose the GST rate applicable.
Step 3: Select whether the amount is inclusive or exclusive of GST.
