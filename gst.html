GST Calculator

About GST:
GST (Goods and Services Tax) ek indirect tax hai jo India me 1 July 2017 se lagu hua. Iska main aim tha alag-alag taxes ko hata kar ek single tax system lana. GST ke andar Central GST (CGST), State GST (SGST), aur Integrated GST (IGST) aate hain.

Jab aap kisi item ya service ko kharidte hain to GST include hota hai. Agar aap 'Add GST' choose karte hain to total amount me GST jod diya jata hai. 'Remove GST' ka matlab hai ki kisi given total price se GST ka hissa alag kiya jaye.

Is calculator ki madad se aap asaani se kisi bhi amount par GST add ya minus kar sakte hain.
