Formulas Used:

  • X% of Y = (X/100) × Y
  • Percentage of X from Y = (X/Y) × 100
  • X is Y% of Z ⇒ Z = (X × 100) / Y
  • Percentage Change = [(New - Old) / Old] × 100

Example Calculations

  • What is 25% of 200? → 50
  • 40 is what percent of 500? → 8%
  • 70 is 35% of what? → 200
  • Old: 120, New: 150 → 25% Increase
