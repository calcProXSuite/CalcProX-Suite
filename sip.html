Investment Summary

What is a SIP Calculator?

A SIP calculator helps you estimate the returns on your mutual fund investments made through a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP). It allows you to plan your monthly contributions and forecast the growth of your investments over a specific period.

How does SIP work?

SIP allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in a mutual fund scheme. The investments are made at periodic intervals (monthly, quarterly), helping average out the cost and benefit from compounding over time.

Benefits of SIP Investment

  • Disciplined investing
  • Rupee cost averaging
  • Power of compounding
  • Flexible and convenient

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Can SIP make you rich?
A: SIPs may build wealth over time through disciplined investing and compounding, but results depend on market performance.

Q2: Is SIP better than FD?
A: SIPs typically offer higher returns but with risk, while FDs are safer but lower-yielding.
