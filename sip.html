What is SIP?

A SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a disciplined method of investing a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. With SIP, investors benefit from rupee cost averaging and the power of compounding.

How does a SIP Calculator help?

  • Estimates maturity amount based on input values
  • Helps visualize wealth gained vs investment
  • Assists in planning long-term financial goals

Formula Used

The SIP maturity formula is:
M = P × [(1 + r)^n – 1] × (1 + r)/r
Where:
M = Maturity Amount
P = SIP Amount
r = Monthly Interest Rate
n = Number of Months

Example

If you invest ₹5,000 per month for 10 years with 12% annual return, maturity = ₹11,61,695

Benefits of SIP

  • Disciplined savings
  • Rupee cost averaging
  • Power of compounding
  • Flexible investment options

FAQs

Can SIPs give guaranteed returns?

No. Returns depend on market performance. SIP is a tool to manage volatility.

Is SIP only for long-term?

Best suited for long-term wealth creation, but short-term SIPs are also possible.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes. SIPs can be started, paused, or stopped anytime without penalty.
